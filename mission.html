Our Mission & Expertise

Our Commitment to Legal Precision

At ClaimWrit, clarity of purpose, structural precision, and prompt resolution undergird our legal support services. We are dedicated to equipping counsel with meticulously drafted demand letters and comprehensive post-settlement solutions engineered to streamline workflow, optimize client recoveries, and expedite the orderly resolution of claims.

Our Expertise

  • Strategic Demand-Letter Preparation — Fully litigation-ready demand letters integrating liability analysis with precise damages computations. Every medical chronology is reviewed to ensure all documented injuries, diagnostic findings, and physician observations are accurately reflected.
  • Post-Settlement Medical Reduction Requests — We analyze subrogation, health-insurance EOBs, and statutory liens to secure the most favorable reductions for clients.
  • Client Balance Verification & Closing Statements — Detailed balance verifications and closing statements crafted with financial accuracy for smooth file closure.

Quality Control: Every Document, Double-Checked

Every deliverable undergoes a dual-phase QA process—ensuring accuracy, persuasiveness, and error-free copy.

Flexible Engagement & Pricing

ClaimWrit supports flat-fee demand-letter drafting or hourly post-settlement services—tailored to your firm’s needs.

Trusted Legal Support for Law Firms

ClaimWrit serves as a steadfast partner to counsel—empowering attorneys with expert drafting, operational support, and streamlined claim-closure solutions.
