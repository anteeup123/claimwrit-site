Why the Demand Letter Matters

A compelling personal injury demand letter does more than outline damages—it sets the tone. By establishing liability, presenting supporting evidence, and framing the narrative with clarity, it helps drive settlement negotiations with purpose. When structured strategically, these letters often result in faster, fairer resolutions—maximizing compensation while reducing the need for litigation.
