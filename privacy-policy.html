Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2024

ClaimWrit, a d/b/a of Envisage Companies LLC, is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with HIPAA and applicable Florida law, including FIPA. This policy outlines what data we collect, how we protect it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Identifying Information (e.g., name, law firm, phone number, email address)
  • Protected Health Information (PHI) uploaded to our client portal
  • Usage details and communication history

How We Collect It

We collect data through secure website forms, our client-upload portal, and direct correspondence such as phone, email, or intake calls.

How We Protect It

All data is encrypted using TLS during transmission and AES-256 at rest. Access is limited to authorized personnel only. Search engine indexing is blocked to help protect submitted documents.

How We Use It

We use submitted data exclusively to fulfill your request for services, including demand-letter drafting, lien reductions, and follow-up communications. We do not share or sell your information.

Third-Party Services

We may use secure third-party platforms (such as Netlify or WordPress plugins) to deliver services. These services are contractually obligated to comply with data protection requirements.

Analytics & Cookies

This site may use cookies or analytics tools to improve performance and usability. These tools do not collect or process PHI.

Retention Period

We retain uploaded or submitted data for the duration of your engagement and archive it afterward in accordance with applicable retention requirements.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information by contacting legal@claimwrit.com.

Breach Notification

ClaimWrit follows HIPAA and FIPA breach-notification requirements. In the rare event of a covered incident, clients will be notified within 30 days in accordance with applicable laws.

Children’s Privacy

This site is not intended for individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. All updates will be posted to this page with a revised effective date.
