How ClaimWrit Supports Attorneys—Step by Step

From intake to delivery, here’s how ClaimWrit transforms case documents into litigation-ready support.

Step 1: Submit Your Case Files

Upload medical records, billing statements, and incident details through our secure, encrypted client-upload portal—fast, organized, and compliant.

Step 2: Personal Case Review

Each file receives a thorough manual review—assessing timeline accuracy, treatment patterns, provider types, and potential inconsistencies. No black-box automation.

Step 3: Structure Into a Clear Timeline

Treatment notes, radiology reports, procedures, and summaries are distilled into a coherent chronology—focused on the facts that matter.

Step 4: Billing Is Summarized

We provide an itemized tabulation under “Injuries & Damages” so adjusters and opposing counsel see a crystal-clear financial overview.

Step 5: Demand Letter Delivered

You receive an editable, professionally formatted PDF—courtroom-ready and tailored for negotiation. It includes:

  • I. Facts & Liability
  • II. Injuries & Damages
  • III. Summary & Demand
